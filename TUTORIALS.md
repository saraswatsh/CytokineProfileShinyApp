## Exploratory Analysis Guides

Choose these when your goal is to inspect distributions, group summaries, effect-size screens, correlation structure, or clustering before moving into more formal modeling.

Choose this when you want to see raw distribution shape, spread, and possible outliers before picking a statistical test:

* [Understanding Boxplots and Violin Plots](https://shinyinfo.cytokineprofile.org/articles/Understanding-Boxplots-and-Violin-Plots.html)

Choose this when you want compact group summaries with uncertainty bars across many cytokines:

* [Understanding Error-Bar Plots](https://shinyinfo.cytokineprofile.org/articles/Understanding-Error-Bar-Plot.html)

Choose this when you want two-group differential screening with fold-change and p-value emphasis:

* [Understanding Volcano Plot](https://shinyinfo.cytokineprofile.org/articles/Understanding-Volcano-Plot.html)

Choose this when you want two-group effect-size screening with SSMD plus fold-change thresholds:

* [Understanding Dual-Flashlight Plot](https://shinyinfo.cytokineprofile.org/articles/Understanding-Dual-Flashlight-Plot.html)

Choose this when you want unsupervised clustering or a matrix-style overview of many samples and cytokines:

* [Understanding Heatmaps](https://shinyinfo.cytokineprofile.org/articles/Understanding-Heatmaps.html)

Choose this when your main question is about coordinated behavior among numeric variables rather than mean differences:

* [Understanding Correlation Analysis](https://shinyinfo.cytokineprofile.org/articles/Understanding-Correlation-Analysis.html)
